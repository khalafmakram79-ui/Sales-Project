# Services Dataset - Power BI & SQL Dashboard

## Project Summary
Interactive business intelligence dashboard built to analyze a consulting firm's service performance. 
The project leverages **SQL for data extraction and transformation** and **Power BI for visualization** to provide actionable insights on revenue, client performance, and regional contribution.

## Business Problem
Help leadership track revenue drivers, identify top-performing regions/departments, and monitor client contributions to optimize resource allocation and business growth.

## Key Achievements
- **$195.3M Total Revenue** analyzed across 5 service departments
- **216,782 Total Hours** tracked and visualized
- Identified **Europe as top region** contributing 37.73% of total revenue
- Built dynamic filters to enable self-service analysis by **Month, Quarter, and Region**

## Technical Tools & Skills
- **SQL**: Data cleaning, joins, aggregations, and KPI calculations
- **Power BI**: Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive dashboards, slicers
- **Power Query**: ETL process and data transformation
- **Data Visualization**: Bar charts, Donut charts, Line charts, Tables, KPI cards

## Dashboard Features
1.  **KPI Cards**: Total Revenue and Total Hours with real-time values
2.  **Department Analysis**: Revenue breakdown across Advisory, Audit, Legal, Management, Tax
3.  **Regional Performance**: Donut + Bar chart showing Europe, Americas, APAC, EMEA contribution
4.  **Time Trend**: Revenue trend analysis from Jan 2023 to Oct 2023
5.  **Top Clients**: Table showing top 10 clients by revenue
6.  **Interactive Slicers**: Month, Quarter, Region for dynamic filtering

## Dataset
**Source**: Kaggle - Services Dataset  
**Files**: `Services_data.csv`, `Branch_data.csv`, `SQLQuery2.sql`
